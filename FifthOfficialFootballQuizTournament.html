The Ultimate Sports IQ Clash: Fifth Official Football Quiz Tournament

Published by tumusiime.com

KAMPALA, UGANDA – The long-awaited Fifth Official Football Quiz Tournament is officially underway, bringing a new level of competitive spirit to the Ugandan sports community. This unique tournament challenges duos to fight for the top spot using nothing but their Sports IQ.

“We have kicked off this incredible journey. The atmosphere is electric—the boys come here to fight for the top spot, and it has all the craze you can expect from football fanatics. This is the first ever edition, and we are beyond excited.”
— Tumu Siime, Creator & Host.

Tournament Structure

The tournament follows a high-stakes professional format designed to test endurance and knowledge:

  • Group Stage: 24 duos divided into 8 groups.
  • Knockout Phase: The top two duos from each group qualify for the Round of 16.
  • Final Sprint: Winners advance through Quarter-finals and Semi-finals to reach the Grand Final.

What’s at Stake?

Beyond the bragging rights of being the most knowledgeable football fans in the city, the tournament offers significant cash prizes:

  • 1st Place: Ugx 1,000,000
  • 2nd Place: Ugx 500,000
  • 3rd Place: Ugx 250,000

Where to Watch

Fans can follow every match and catch the highlights on the Fifth Official Ug YouTube Channel. Additionally, coverage will be broadcast across all of Tumu Siime’s official media channels.
